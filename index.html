Ashley Grealish

Hi, I’m Ashley, co-founder and CEO of Windfall Energy. While the clean energy transition is accelerating, I noticed that most solutions exclude the millions living in flats and rented homes. That’s why I’m building Windfall Energy - to create affordable, installation-free home batteries that work for everyone.

My journey in clean energy began while studying Engineering at @imperialcollege, where I worked with @e.quinox to bring renewable energy to rural communities in East Africa.

This led me to scale solar solutions across sub-Saharan Africa @bboxx, develop ethical diamond mining technology @GemFair, and most recently, build the world’s leading EV smart charging platform as CTO @ev.energy.
